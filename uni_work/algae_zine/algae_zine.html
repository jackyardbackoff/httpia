For our final DES101 assignment, we were tasked with creating a zine about a design topic of our choosing (though our choices were restricted). I decided to focus my project on algae!!!!

We were asked to cover the past and future of our topic, and discuss its relationship to the principles of Te Tiriti & UoA's design kawa. As this was a final, we had a few weeks for research, planning & delivery. We also printed physical copies!

I lost a lot of hair and sleep over this assignment, but I'm proud of what I made! Layout design was especially fun & I spent many consecutive hours happily stinking out on my laptop.

The written content of this project might be total arse. Please don't read it critically B'(
